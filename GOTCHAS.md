# GOTCHAS

Capture every undocumented surprise discovered during build at the milestone it surfaces. Per `book/PHILOSOPHY.md` "Capture gotchas at every milestone".

Each entry: what surprised, why it surprised, what to do. One paragraph. New entries append; obsolete entries trim only when proven obsolete by verifier run.

## Format

```
### <topic kebab-case>

What surprised: <observation>
Why: <mechanism that caused it>
Fix: <runnable next step>
Caught by: <verifier target that now covers this>
```
